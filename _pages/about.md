Hello! I am currently a doctoral candidate in [I. Physikalisches Institut](https://astro.uni-koeln.de/) at the [Universität zu Köln](https://www.uni-koeln.de/). My research is focused on the development of three-dimensional models 
of photo-dissociation regions (PDRs) using the KOSMA-tau code developed in Cologne. My work is largely based in python. Since python is not the fastest language to use for 
complex astrophysical programs, I spend a great deal of my time optimising code.

Outreach
===

Beyond my efforts in PDR-modelling, I am heavily involved in public outreach. I have started a satellite of [Astronomy on Tap](https://astronomyontap.org) in Köln 
with some other PhD students and postdocs in 2020. Management of [Astronomy on Tap Köln](https://facebook.com/aotkoeln) takes up most of my spare time, but I am always 
looking for new and exciting ways to reach the public. Feel free to contact me if you would like to collaborate!
